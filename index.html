Welcome. We are Port Hacking Lapidary Club, a highly social group of rockhounds and gemstone enthusiasts based in the heart of the Sutherland Shire.

Come for a cuppa, and stay for the opportunity to learn about and participate in the many crafts on offer.



Amenities

Our clubhouse is fully equipped for crafts related to lapidary, the cutting and polishing of gemstones, including cabochoning, faceting, silverwork, jewellery-making, Lost-wax casting, enamelling and carving. We also have a small library of books for club members and a designated break for coffee and biscuits!

Membership
Interested in joining? We would love to have you!

Whether you’re an expert looking for like-minded people or a newbie who just loves gemstones and wants to make something to wear or display, we try to create an open and welcoming environment. With 80+ members, you’re sure to find someone who shares your passion who can show you the ropes.

Our membership is annual and includes personal insurance to cover you during workshop sessions.


Annual Membership Fee: $25.00
Workshop Fee: $6 per session (includes morning or afternoon tea)
